Description:
Koshari is a hearty Egyptian dish combining rice, lentils, pasta, and chickpeas, topped with a tangy tomato-vinegar sauce and crispy fried onions. It's a beloved vegan street food staple.

Ingrediants:

  • Brown lentils
  • Rice
  • Small pasta (macaroni/vermicelli)
  • Chickpeas
  • Onions (for frying)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Ground cumin
  • Oil, salt, and pepper

Steps:

  1. Soak and cook lentils until tender.
  2. Cook rice and pasta separately.
  3. Fry thinly sliced onions until golden and crispy.
  4. Prepare tomato-vinegar sauce with garlic and spices.
  5. Layer rice, lentils, pasta, and chickpeas in a bowl.
  6. Top with tomato sauce and fried onions. Serve hot.
